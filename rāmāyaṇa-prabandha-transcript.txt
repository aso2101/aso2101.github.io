RĀMĀYAṆAPRABANDHA

1.  asti praśastā janalocanānām
    ānanda-sandāyiṣu kosaleṣu |
    ājñā-samutsārita-dānavānāṃ
    rājñām ayodhyeti purī raghūṇāṃ ||
      (indravajrā)

2.  yasyām udagra-nṛpa-mandira-candraśālā-
    vātāyane gativaśād vapuṣi prasaktāṃ |
    dīpāgra-dhūma-maṣikāṃ śiśirāṃśu-biṃbe
    mohāt kuraṃga iti mugdha-janāḥ vadanti || 
      (vasantatilakam)

3.  cikrīṃsayā kṛtrima-patripaṃkteḥ
    kapota-pāḷīṣu niketanānāṃ |
    mārjjāram apy āyata-niścalāṃgaṃ
    yasyāṃ janāḥ kṛtrimam eva mene ||
      (indravajrā)
      (= Māgha 3.51, cikraṃsayā)

4.  tām āvasad daśarathaḥ sura-vanditena
    saṃkrandanena vihitāsana-saṃvibhāgaḥ |
    vṛndārakāri-vijaye sura-loka-labdha-
    mandāra-mālya-madhu-vāsita-vāsa-bhūmiḥ ||
     (vasantatilakam)

5.  sa hi mahīpatir ahīna-supathi-sañcāra-kauśalyayā
    kausalyayā mahīyamāna-saubhāgya-mayyā kaikeyyā nirupa-
    dhika-nija-guṇa-gaṇa-paritoṣita-mitrayā sumitrayā ca tribhir amī-
    bhir abhirūpair anurūpair udārair ddārais saha carita-dharmmā ni-
    rmmāya-puṇya-bhara-vitīrṇṇa-śārṅga-dhara-caraṇa-sarasija-bhakti-nistuṣa-
    matir aparasparavirodha-mahitam artthatritayam aticirāyānvati-
    ṣthat 
     (gadyam)

6.  aneka-bhūpais samadair ahantayā
    parasparākarṣaṇa-tāpa-khedinī |
    sametya yaṃ kīrtti-dukūla-saṃvṛtā
    pati-vratāvṛttim avāpa medinī ||
     (vaṃśastham)
     
7.  samantād ādhūta-vyajana-pavanāsevita-tanu-
    s samantābhiś ceto-haraṇa-caturābhiḥ sutanubhiḥ |
    purārāme ramye kanaka-sadane vā cirataraṃ
    yathākāmaṃ reme bhuvana-tala-nātho daśarathaḥ || 
     (śikhariṇī)

8.  kriyāprabandhād ayam addhvarāṇā
    m ajasram āhūta-sahasra-netraḥ |
    śacyāś ciraṃ pāṇḍu-kapola-laṃbān
    mandāra-śūnyān aḷakāṃś cakāra ||
     (upajātiḥ)

9.  atha narapatir akheṭa-kīrttir ākheṭa-kautukī saciva-
    sanmatas taruṇa-taru-chada-chāya-tanu-chada-chaṃditāṅgaḥ jaṃghā-java-ji
    ta-kuraṃgaṃ turaṃgam adhirūhya sa-niṣaṃga-śarāsano dhanurvveda-pāra-
    dṛśvā viśva-śvāpada-śaraṇyam araṇyam ayāsīt |
     (gadyam)
   
10. kṛntan dantikulaṃ śareṇa gamayan
      pañcānanān pañcatāṃ
    śārdūlān muhur arddayann apadayaṃ
      bhūdārakān dārayan |
    sañchindan śarabhotkaraṃ sarabhasaṃ
      niṣkāsayan kāsarān
    kāntāre vijahāra tuṃgaturagaṃ
      rūḍho dharitrī-patiḥ ||
       (śārdūlavikrīḍitam)

11. tadanu muni-kumāras tatra pitror nnideśāt
    sarasi taru-niruddhe kuṃbham uṃbhāṃ-cakāra |
    ha ha ha sa nihato 'bhūt jāta-kuṃbhi-bhrameṇa
    svayam avanibhujāyaṃ ko vidhiṃ rodhuṃ iṣṭe |
     (mālinī)
 
12. tenāvatīryya turagāt prathitānvayena
    pṛṣṭānvayas sajalakuṃbha-niṣaṇṇa-dehaḥ |
    tasmai dvijetara-tapasvi-sutas skhaladbhi
    r ātmānam akṣarapadaiḥ kathayāṃ babhūva ||
     (vasantatilakam)

12. tau dampatī bahu vilapya śiśoḥ praharttraṃ
    śalyaṃ nikhātam udahārayatām urastaḥ |
    so 'bhūt parāsur atha bhūmi-patiṃ śaśāpa
    hastārppitair nnayana-vāribhir eva vṛddhaḥ ||
     (vasantatilakam)

12. ajñānād api vihitaṃ tvayā sahiṣṇu-
    s saṃsthāsye sutavirahavyathāṃ yathā 'haṃ |
    antye 'pi priya-suta-viprayōgaśokāt
    pañcatvaṃ vayasi tathā tvam āśrayethāḥ ||
     (praharṣiṇī)

13. prāptāntagaḥ sapadi śāsanam asya rājā
    sampādya pātaka-vilupta-dhutir nnivṛttaḥ |
    antar-nniviṣṭa-padam ātma-vināśa-hetuṃ
    śāpaṃ dadhat punar avāpa purīm ayodhyāṃ ||
     (vasantatilakam) 

14. pṛthivīṃ śāsatas tasya pāka-śāsana-tejasaḥ |
    kiñcid ūnam anūna-rddheś śaradām ayutaṃ yayau ||
     (anuṣṭubh)

15. tadanu vasumatiṃ payodhākāñcīṃ (read payodha)
    bhuja-vibhavena nijena pālayantaṃ |
    anudinam anapatyatā narendraṃ
    manasi dadāha śikhīva pādapendraṃ ||
      (puṣpitāgrā)

16. digaṃbaraṃ gatavrīḷaṃ jaṭilaṃ dhūḷi-dhūsaraṃ |
    kṣīṇa-puṇyā na paśyanti maheśvaram ivātmajaṃ ||
      (anuṣṭubh)

17. ālakṣya-danta-mukaḷān animitta-hāsai
    r aspaṣṭa-varṇṇa-ramaṇīya-vacaḥ-pravṛttīn |
    aṅgāśraya-praṇayinas tanayān vahantō
    dhanyās tad-aṃga-rajasā malinī bhavanti ||
     (vasantatilakam)

18. kiṅkiṇī-jāla-lasitān
    kaṅkaṇair aṅkitān kare |
    tān bālān avalokyārāt 
    prītiṃ prāpsyāmy ahaṃ kadā ||
     (anuṣṭubh)
