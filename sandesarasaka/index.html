Instructions

The text is from: Muni Jinavijaya and Harivallabh C. Bhayani (eds.), Sandeśarāsaka of Abdul Rahman. Singhi Jain Series no. 2.. Bombay: Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, 1945. This was edited from three manuscripts, which they call A, B, and C

For every verse, there may be one of any of the following five buttons on the right-hand side:

  • displays information about the metrical pattern of the verse.
  • displays the ṭippanaka commentary from manuscript B of the text.
  • displays the avacūrikā commentary from manuscript A of the text. (In most cases, the two commentaries are nearly identical.)
  • displays my own working translation of the text.
  • displays some notes, including a glossary.

This electronic text was put together by Andrew Ollett in June 2017.
